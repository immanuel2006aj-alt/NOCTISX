COOKIE POLICY

Understanding How Cookies Work

This page explains what cookies are, why they are used and how you can control your cookie preferences while using Noctis Technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Why We Use Cookies

Cookies may be used to improve website performance, remember preferences and understand how visitors interact with our website.

Types Of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies
  • Performance Cookies
  • Analytics Cookies
  • Preference Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

If analytics tools are added in the future, they may collect anonymous usage information to improve the website experience.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to view, block or delete cookies through browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect website functionality.

Cookie Preferences

When available, you can choose whether to allow optional cookies. Essential cookies remain active because they are required for the website to operate correctly.

Third-Party Services

Some services integrated into the website may set their own cookies. Examples may include analytics, embedded maps, videos or payment providers. These services operate under their own privacy and cookie policies.

How Long Cookies Are Stored

Cookie duration varies depending on their purpose. Some cookies exist only during your visit, while others may remain on your device until they expire or are removed.

Browser Controls

Most modern browsers allow you to:

  • View stored cookies.
  • Delete cookies.
  • Block specific websites.
  • Disable all cookies.
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored.

Policy Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated whenever new services or technologies are introduced. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Questions

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy or website privacy, please contact us through our Contact page.
