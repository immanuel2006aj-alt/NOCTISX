Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Noctis Technologies respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you voluntarily provide through this website.

We may collect information that you choose to submit through contact forms, project enquiry forms or newsletter subscriptions.

  • Business Information (if provided)
  • Project Details

How Information Is Used

Information is used only for:

Essential cookies may be used to improve website functionality. Optional cookies will only be used after your consent where applicable.

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, where applicable.
